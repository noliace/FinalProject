About Us

Inspired by the Lyceum of Aparri's mission, the High School Department is committed to provide an atmosphere of education that facilitates the integral
development of its students through its Christian/Diocesan - inspired and Filipino value - oriented curricular and co - curricular activities in order to empower them to
effect transformation in the school, in the home, in the community, and in the environment.
